PROFESSIONAL SKILLS
UX, Web Design & Frontend Development

ProfessionalSkills is an online Learning service that LearningWorks provides for the corporate sector. They required a complete overhaul of the previous site, which included an update of the design and User Experience, addition of e-commerce, SEO, account creation, Wordpress integration, and Moodle LMS integration - all of which needed to be fully responsive.

My tasks were to consider and plan an optimum User Experience through user research, wireframing and prototyping, and to create an inviting and up-to-date website design that could stand alone whilst still obviously being a part of the LearningWorks franchise. I also did responsive Frontend Development, using SCSS with Compass and Susy Grids, and HTML.
