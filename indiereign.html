INDIEREIGN
Multidisciplinary

IndieReign is a web-based startup founded by David White. It provides independent filmmakers with the tools they need to self-distribute their films, with a player specially designed for instant purchase, in accordance with today's user demands of instant internet based access to entertainment media.

As I was with the company from day one of foundation, and remained for three and a half years, my tasks and roles spanned a large variety of needs like: business and digital strategy, print design, branding, user experience, website design, frontend development, iPad and Android app design, and more.


All of the work shown below is mine, starting from brief establishment, strategy, user research, interaction design, and finally frontend development and hand-off.

    • PROJECT HIGHLIGHT

    For the following project, I was tasked with incentivising IndieReign's users to promote their films. To fully understand what motivates IndieReign's users, I talked to the Customer Service team, and our resident Filmmaker, Mike, about their observations of user behaviour.

    I learned that Filmmakers are largely intrinsictly motivated; they love to be appreciated and to see that people love their work, and that their work is being promoted and rewarded. There was little to no care for extrinsic motivators, like money rewards. I also learned that Filmmakers don't really know how to market and promote their films.

    So, I created a reward system that would mean we give special notice to those who put the most effort into promoting their films, as well as rewards that emphasised the benefit to them, if they promoted their film, with tips on how.
