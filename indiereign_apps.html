INDIEREIGN APP
App Design

Due to the scale and type of services IndieReign supplies, they required a native app, for both Apple and Android. I was tasked with the entire strategy, planning, UX, and GUI process, before handing it all over to the different Developers for Development.

    • STRATEGY

    To start, I researched the user base of both Android and Apple tablets, as well as the typical use case scenarios of tablet usage.

    I found that Android users don't like to use products that look like they were first made for Apple products, and then "ported" to Android. This — coupled with Android's design styleguides, that differ from Apple's — meant that the app would not look completly the same across Tablet OS's, as displayed above.

You can download the Android app at the Google Play store .
